Motion Graphic Design for Pittsburgh Penguins, NHL

May.2016 - Dec.2016

My Role

As a motion graphic designer for the Pittsburgh Penguins, I created animations with the game media production team for display on the jumbotron, halo boards and 360Ëš fascia boards in the arena.

Method

To create motion graphics that reflect the Penguin's branding, it was important to follow the style guideline fundamentals I created.

A consistent visual style on each board held the key to send the right message to the audience at any given moment. To accomplish this, I started with crafting the theme for the main board first, then I proceeded to share these focal elements with the Halo and Fascia boards. In terms of the production pipeline, I made 2D/3D animations with After Effects and Cinema 4D. Next, I integrated and composited the assets, and made additional effects in After Effects.

Visiting Starters

Visiting Starters animations show upcoming team match ups for the Penguins. One challenge here was to make a consistent animation for over 30 teams (the match ups that the Pens would face in the NHL season). Creating a template was extremely important here, as it allowed me to create all the animations quickly, swapping out team logos with ease.
